IT and digital skills | Level 2

Description:

The level two IT course is for our students who have some basic knowledge, but want to improve
even more. There is a wide range of knowledge you can gain from and progress further.
You can expect work experience
to build on your work experience.

Duration:

8 months

Key Topics:

  • Online world with IT
  • Database
  • Execel Software
  • Optimising IT performance
  • Web Development
  • Basic Programming
  • Introduction to IT security

Entry Requirments:

Minimum of 3 GCSEs
or 2 GCSES with a level 1 IT qualification
