BTEC Level 3 National Diploma in IT

Description:

BTEC level 3 is widly known accross in the UK.
This course is equivalent to two A levels.
This course is suitbale for students who are interested in progressing to further study in
IT-related disciplines.
IT systems have a significant role in the world around us and play a part in almost everything we do. This course will help you develop the skills required to work in the IT industry through
a range of practical and
skills-based
study methods. The course aims to enable you to
develop both personally and professionally by providing opportunity to enhance
enquiry and investigation skills in solving complex problems both individually and in collaborative environments.

Duration:

8 months

Key Topics:

Year 1

  • Unit 1: Information technology system
  • Unit 2: Creating System to manage information
  • Unit 3 : Social media in business
  • Unit 6: Web Devolepment

Year 2

  • Unit 11: Cyber-Security
  • Unit 9: IT project management
  • Unit 8 : Computer games development
  • Unit 4: Programming

Entry Requirments:

Minimum of 5 GCSEs (Grade 4 -9)
